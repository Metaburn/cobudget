About Cobudget

Cobudget helps organisations and groups allocate funds collaboratively and transparently.

The idea is simple: group members receive funds in their cobudget account. They can propose projects or activities and allocate their own funds to support those of others within the group.

getting started

Cobudget is the flagship product of Greaterthan, a venture that is bringing collaborative finance practices into organizations to help them increase engagement, transparency and agility.
